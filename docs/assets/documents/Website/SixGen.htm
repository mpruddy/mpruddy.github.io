Photo of 

José Romaguera y Roura and his family

Born June 4, 1875 in Guayama, Puerto Rico.

[Son of Pedro Miguel Romaguera y Pruneda and Ana Roura y Tort the immigrants from Catalunya]

In the picture are his son José Mariano Sabas Romaguera y Díaz standing on the far left

[ the great grandfather of Michael Gerard Ruddy y Romaguera]

The rest pictured  right to left are [seated] Arturo Romaguera y Díaz, [standing] José "Papitín" Romaguera y Roura the father,

Hortensia Romaguera y Díaz and Carmen "Cambu" Romaguera y Díaz.

[The children´s mother Mariana (Diaz) Romaguera died before 1910 with the children still young]

I estimate the picture to have been taken between 1920 and 1925
