Clan na Gael Camps

and

Members names gathered from research documents

By Michael Ruddy

 

Definitions used:

DPB = Devoy's Post Bag

GA  = Gaelic American Newspaper

MS###### = Manuscript from the Devoy collection in Ireland

Over a period of years I have been keeping a list of Camps and members of those camps mentioned in the various books and documents I have read. This list such as it is represents this accumulation. I do not have names beyond those given and I do not have biographical information on the members mentioned so from a genealogical standpoint this list is a mere index which tell someone searching a name that somewhere after 1867 and before 1878.
