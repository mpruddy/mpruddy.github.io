INÉS CAMACHO ALBUM SECTION III
Plates 11-20

 

 

Plate 11
Venancio Calle y Sra. + Inés Camacho de V
Note: on back left photo is written "Recuerdo Fraternal Vno. A. Calle Medn. Febo 24 of 1868." [Medn is, probably, Medellín]
There is a bridge from barrio Guayquil to Medellín named for a Don Venancio Calle - could this be his photo?.
Note: on back right photo is written "Ines Camacho de Vallejo a Moices La Torre en prueba de la estimación que le profesa. Calí 29 de Junio de 1881" 

Plate 12
Fidel Lalinde + Selio Flecher 
Note: In 1898 one of the Calí town council members was Don Fidel Lalinde who was a businessman who owned significant property in the city of Calí - this may be his photo.
Note: In the photo on the right there is a variant spelling "Flecher" instead of the expected Flétcher. Nothing is known of this person Selio Flecher.
